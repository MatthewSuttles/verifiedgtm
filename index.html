Why this exists

We needed to know which channels deserved a week of founder time, so we did the unglamorous thing: opened every review page, read every thread, and wrote down the date we checked.

Two things became obvious. First, most GTM advice isn't wrong, it's unverified. Nobody checked whether the channel still works for your category, this year. Second, a verified dead end is worth as much as a verified winner, because it's a week you get to keep.

So that's the product. Not advice. Checking. Someone finally opens every page, reads every thread, and writes down what's actually there, for your app specifically.
